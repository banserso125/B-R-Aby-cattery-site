Our Heritage

A Journey of Dedication.

We are a Hong Kong Abyssinian cattery registered under the Cat Fanciers' Association (CFA). Our fascination with this mystical breed was love at first sight. It all started with our neutered pet Abyssinian girl—Dristi.

The more we studied and learned from professional catteries around the world, the more irresistible it became. Since then, it has become a lifelong journey of dedication to this ancient and mystical breed.
